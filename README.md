# E-Commerce-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-
This project features an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize e-commerce sales data.
The primary goal is to provide actionable insights into business performance, helping stakeholders track key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions to optimize revenue.

1. Dashboard Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of E-Commerce Sales Performance, focusing on:
•	Total Revenue: 302M 
•	Total Orders: 10K 
•	Average Order Value: 30.2K 
•	Total Units Sold: 30K 
•	Average Rating: 3.01 
It analyzes performance across:
•	Product Categories 
•	Platforms (Amazon, Jumia, Souq) 
•	Cities 
•	Brands & Products 
•	Customer Reviews & Ratings 
•	Time-based Sales Trends 
________________________________________
📊 2. Key Insights
🔹 Category Performance
•	Electronics (30.57%) and Fashion (30.3%) dominate revenue. 
•	Computers (19%) contribute moderately. 
•	Accessories & Wearables (~20%) have lower contribution. 
👉 Insight:
Revenue is heavily concentrated in 2 main categories, indicating dependency risk.
________________________________________
🔹 Platform Analysis
•	Souq (3.37K products) slightly leads. 
•	Jumia (3.34K) and Amazon (3.29K) are almost equal. 
👉 Insight:
All platforms perform almost equally, showing a balanced distribution strategy.
________________________________________
🔹 City-wise Sales
Top performing cities:
•	Cairo (52.52M) 
•	Casablanca (52.38M) 
•	Riyadh (50.36M) 
Lower:
•	Alexandria (48.84M) 
👉 Insight:
Sales are well distributed geographically, with no extreme dependency on a single city.
________________________________________
🔹 Brand & Category Count
•	Electronics & Fashion: 3K brands each 
•	Computers: 2K 
•	Accessories & Wearables: 1K each 
👉 Insight:
More brands = higher competition → impacts pricing & margins.
________________________________________
🔹 Product Performance
Top products:
•	Xiaomi products: 34M revenue 
•	Smartwatch, Nike Air, T-shirt: ~31M each 
👉 Insight:
Few products generate high revenue concentration (top-heavy sales).
________________________________________
🔹 Reviews vs Ratings
•	Reviews range: 2.3M – 2.6M 
•	Ratings mostly around 3.0 
👉 Insight:
High engagement but average satisfaction level (rating ~3) → improvement needed.
________________________________________
🔹 Sales Trend Over Time
•	Sales fluctuate throughout the year. 
•	No consistent upward growth trend. 
👉 Insight:
Sales are unstable and seasonal, not consistently growing.
________________________________________
📌 3. Conclusion
•	Business is performing strongly with 302M revenue, but growth is not stable. 
•	Revenue is concentrated in few categories and products. 
•	Customer engagement is high, but satisfaction is average. 
•	Platform and geographic distribution is well balanced. 
•	Growth opportunity exists in low-performing categories and customer experience.
